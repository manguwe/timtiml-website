Cambridge / ZIMSEC

O Level Computer Science

O Level Computer Science introduces you to the fundamentals of how computers work — from binary logic to writing your first programs. Whether you're studying Cambridge IGCSE or the Zambian/Zimbabwean curriculum, this course builds a rock-solid foundation for further studies and a career in tech.

  • Data representation — binary, hex, and character encoding
  • Computer hardware — CPU, memory, storage, I/O devices
  • Networking basics — LANs, WANs, the internet
  • Programming fundamentals — pseudocode and flowcharts
  • Algorithms — sorting, searching, and problem-solving
  • Cybersecurity — threats, malware, and safe practices
  • Database concepts — tables, queries, and SQL basics
  • Software development lifecycle

Advanced Level

A Level Computer Science

A Level Computer Science takes you deep into the theory and practice of computing. You'll explore complex algorithms, data structures, object-oriented programming, and computational thinking at a level that prepares you for university-level computer science and professional software development.

  • Advanced algorithms — complexity, Big O notation
  • Data structures — stacks, queues, trees, graphs, hash tables
  • Object-oriented programming — classes, inheritance, polymorphism
  • Functional programming concepts
  • Computer architecture — Von Neumann model, fetch-execute cycle
  • Operating systems — scheduling, memory management
  • Databases — relational theory, normalisation, SQL
  • Boolean algebra and logic circuits
  • Artificial intelligence introduction

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out. Can't find your answer here? Ask Anotida directly on WhatsApp.

How long does a design take? +
Simple designs like social media graphics or flyers are usually ready within 2–6 hours. More complex work like logos or brand packages may take 1–3 days. Rush delivery is available — mention it when ordering.
What do I need to provide when ordering a design? +
The more detail the better! Share: type of design (poster, logo, etc.), purpose / occasion, preferred colours, any text to include, and reference images you like. Use the order form or WhatsApp directly.
What format will I receive my design in? +
Designs are delivered as high-quality PNG or JPG via WhatsApp. For logos, PDF and SVG versions are also available on request. Print-ready files (300 DPI) are provided for physical print orders.
Can I request revisions? +
Yes — up to 2 free revisions are included per order. Additional revisions may have a small fee depending on the changes requested. Anotida wants you to be happy with the result!
How do the online lessons work? +
Each course has 7–9 lessons with real code examples, quizzes and copy buttons. Lessons are sequential — you must pass the quiz (85%+) on each lesson before unlocking the next. Lessons 1–4 are free. Lesson 5 requires a free email sign-up. Lesson 6 onwards requires a one-time payment.
What score do I need to pass a quiz? +
You need 85% or above (3 out of 4 questions correct). If you don't pass, you can retry immediately. There's no limit on retries — just re-read the lesson and try again!
Do I get a certificate when I finish? +
Yes! Once you complete all lessons and pass all quizzes in a course, a "Download My Certificate" button appears. It generates a personalised, printable certificate with your name, the course name, date and Anotida's signature. You can save it as a PDF using your browser's print function.
Will my progress be saved if I close the browser? +
Yes — progress is saved in your browser's local storage automatically. It will be there when you return on the same device and browser. Note: clearing your browser data or using a different device will reset progress.
What payment methods are accepted? +
MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, EcoCash, Bank Transfer, PayPal (friends & family), Mukuru and Western Union. Multiple currencies accepted — ZMW, USD, ZWL, ZAR, GBP and more. Select your currency when submitting the payment form.
How do I prove I've paid? +
After filling the payment form, you'll be directed to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message including your reference code. Take a screenshot of your payment and send it to Anotida on WhatsApp. Once confirmed, Anotida will send you an unlock code to access the premium lessons.
How quickly will I get my unlock code after paying? +
Usually within 1 hour during business hours (8am–8pm CAT). Anotida will send your unlock code via WhatsApp as soon as he sees and confirms your payment screenshot.
Will my website work on mobile? +
Absolutely — every website is fully mobile responsive. It will look great on phones, tablets and desktops. This is a standard requirement, not an add-on.
Will I own the website after it's built? +
Yes — you receive the full source code. The site is deployed to a free Vercel URL by default. If you want a custom domain (e.g. yourbusiness.com), Anotida can set that up — domain registration is an additional cost (~$10/year).
Can you update my website after it's launched? +
Yes — ongoing maintenance and content updates are available as a paid service. Minor text/image updates are often done quickly and affordably. Contact Anotida to discuss a maintenance arrangement.
Where is Anotida based? +
Anotida is based in Zambia 🇿🇲 (studying at Eden University) and serves clients across Zambia, Zimbabwe and internationally. All work is delivered digitally via WhatsApp and email, so location is no barrier.
How do I contact Anotida? +

O Level Computer Science introduces you to the fundamentals of how computers work — from binary logic to writing your first programs. Whether you're studying Cambridge IGCSE or the Zambian/Zimbabwean curriculum, this course builds a rock-solid foundation for further studies and a career in tech.

  • Data representation — binary, hex, and character encoding
  • Computer hardware — CPU, memory, storage, I/O devices

  • Programming fundamentals — pseudocode and flowcharts
  • Algorithms — sorting, searching, and problem-solving
  • Cybersecurity — threats, malware, and safe practices
  • Database concepts — tables, queries, and SQL basics
  • Software development lifecycle

Advanced Level

A Level Computer Science

A Level Computer Science takes you deep into the theory and practice of computing. You'll explore complex algorithms, data structures, object-oriented programming, and computational thinking at a level that prepares you for university-level computer science and professional software development.

  • Advanced algorithms — complexity, Big O notation
  • Data structures — stacks, queues, trees, graphs, hash tables
  • Object-oriented programming — classes, inheritance, polymorphism
  • Functional programming concepts
  • Computer architecture — Von Neumann model, fetch-execute cycle
  • Operating systems — scheduling, memory management
  • Databases — relational theory, normalisation, SQL
  • Boolean algebra and logic circuits
  • Artificial intelligence introduction

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out. Can't find your answer here? Ask Anotida directly on WhatsApp.

How long does a design take? +
Simple designs like social media graphics or flyers are usually ready within 2–6 hours. More complex work like logos or brand packages may take 1–3 days. Rush delivery is available — mention it when ordering.
What do I need to provide when ordering a design? +
The more detail the better! Share: type of design (poster, logo, etc.), purpose / occasion, preferred colours, any text to include, and reference images you like. Use the order form or WhatsApp directly.
What format will I receive my design in? +
Designs are delivered as high-quality PNG or JPG via WhatsApp. For logos, PDF and SVG versions are also available on request. Print-ready files (300 DPI) are provided for physical print orders.
Can I request revisions? +
Yes — up to 2 free revisions are included per order. Additional revisions may have a small fee depending on the changes requested. Anotida wants you to be happy with the result!
How do the online lessons work? +
Each course has 7–9 lessons with real code examples, quizzes and copy buttons. Lessons are sequential — you must pass the quiz (85%+) on each lesson before unlocking the next. Lessons 1–4 are free. Lesson 5 requires a free email sign-up. Lesson 6 onwards requires a one-time payment.
What score do I need to pass a quiz? +
You need 85% or above (3 out of 4 questions correct). If you don't pass, you can retry immediately. There's no limit on retries — just re-read the lesson and try again!
Do I get a certificate when I finish? +
Yes! Once you complete all lessons and pass all quizzes in a course, a "Download My Certificate" button appears. It generates a personalised, printable certificate with your name, the course name, date and Anotida's signature. You can save it as a PDF using your browser's print function.
Will my progress be saved if I close the browser? +
Yes — progress is saved in your browser's local storage automatically. It will be there when you return on the same device and browser. Note: clearing your browser data or using a different device will reset progress.
What payment methods are accepted? +
MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, EcoCash, Bank Transfer, PayPal (friends & family), Mukuru and Western Union. Multiple currencies accepted — ZMW, USD, ZWL, ZAR, GBP and more. Select your currency when submitting the payment form.
How do I prove I've paid? +
After filling the payment form, you'll be directed to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message including your reference code. Take a screenshot of your payment and send it to Anotida on WhatsApp. Once confirmed, Anotida will send you an unlock code to access the premium lessons.
How quickly will I get my unlock code after paying? +
Usually within 1 hour during business hours (8am–8pm CAT). Anotida will send your unlock code via WhatsApp as soon as he sees and confirms your payment screenshot.
Will my website work on mobile? +
Absolutely — every website is fully mobile responsive. It will look great on phones, tablets and desktops. This is a standard requirement, not an add-on.
Will I own the website after it's built? +
Yes — you receive the full source code. The site is deployed to a free Vercel URL by default. If you want a custom domain (e.g. yourbusiness.com), Anotida can set that up — domain registration is an additional cost (~$10/year).
Can you update my website after it's launched? +
Yes — ongoing maintenance and content updates are available as a paid service. Minor text/image updates are often done quickly and affordably. Contact Anotida to discuss a maintenance arrangement.

Anotida is based in Zambia 🇿🇲 (studying at Eden University) and serves clients across Zambia, Zimbabwe and internationally. All work is delivered digitally via WhatsApp and email, so location is no barrier.
